--- layout: release title: Devastation id: 4 comments: true --- Devastation is the fourth single released under alpha.zero and the second collaboration project between 1oolm and Stretched Across Infinity.

The single was released digitally on September 28th, 2023 on 6 streaming platforms.

This release is followed by Constraint (High Mass Remix).





Trivia


  • This song was originally meant for an EP called FACTOR Y. The remnants of that project can be seen in the cover art, with "FACTORY#4" written above the "DEVASTATION" text, and the wrench-in-hand image itself being on FACTOR Y's cover.
  • This song was made entirely on a phone.
