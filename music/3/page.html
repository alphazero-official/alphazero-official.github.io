--- layout: release title: tomorrowcave id: 3 --- tomorrowcave is the third single released under alpha.zero and the second solo project by 1oolm.

The single was released digitally on September 21st, 2023 on 6 streaming platforms.

This release is followed by Devastation.





Trivia


  • The original iteration of this song was a hardcore track called Bloodied Ice Cubes. It hasn't gotten past the first drop in production, leading to tomorrowcave being salvaged out of the main melody.
  • This song was made entirely on a phone.

Personal Experience: 1oolm


Background


So, a week or two after Constraint's release, my laptop charger melts completely, voiding my PC privileges, then a few days later my phone suffers the same fate. We quickly bought a replacement phone, and I started working on the streaming releases that happened September 3rd.
After that whole shebang, I got bored. So bored that I went back to my roots of FL Studio Mobile. I started off using this app back in Winter 2021-2022, and I haven't touched it since. For good reason! But alas, the devil's pitchfork had to be used.
