--- layout: release title: UNDEFINABLE id: 7 comments: true --- UNDEFINABLE is the seventh single released under alpha.zero and the third collaboration project between 1oolm and Stretched Across Infinity.

The single was released digitally on March 8th, 2024 on 6 streaming platforms.


The track can be considered a detour from 1oolm's usual style of the time, featuring a lesser focus on hardcore drops, instead opting for a more experimental glitch and sampled flavor, derived from Stretched Across Infinity's contributions.

Trivia
